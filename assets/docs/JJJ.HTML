The Problem

Student Housing is Broken

Finding student accommodation in Nigeria is a nightmare. With universities housing less than 30% of students, thousands are forced into an unorganized off-campus market every semester. Students waste weeks searching through unreliable agents, dealing with hidden costs, visiting unsuitable properties, and risking scams. The process is stressful, time-consuming, and often results in overpriced housing far from campus. This chaos affects academic performance, drains finances, and leaves students vulnerable to exploitation.
